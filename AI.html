What is Artificial Intelligence?

Artificial Intelligence makes it possible for machines to learn, perform tasks, and adjust to new inputs. It is called AI for short. Machine learning is a sub-category of AI. Works by collecting data, making an algoethrim, trying that algoethrim, collecting feedback, and applying feedback.

History:

  • term first used in 1965
  • US Department of Defense took interest in 1960
  • Defense Advanced Research Project Agency (DARPA) conducted projects in 1970
  • DARPA produced personal assistants in 2003
